Bear Mayer  

Founder of the Bruce Bolt batting glove compony

Bear Mayer is the founder of Bruce Bolt, a family-owned and operated business based in Austin, Texas. Born in 2001, Bear began his journey with Bruce Bolt at the age of 16, driven by a desire for better batting gloves and the need for funds to purchase his first truck, a 1993 Land Cruiser named Jennifer. His entrepreneurial spirit was ignited by a simple yet profound question: "How can it be better?" This question became the guiding principle behind everything Bear does, from designing the best batting gloves in baseball to questioning every aspect of his business operations, including materials, craftsmanship, packaging, and even how to give back to the baseball community. Bear's commitment to excellence and continuous improvement is reflected in the high-quality, long-lasting equipment produced by Bruce Bolt, backed by exceptional customer service. His vision extends beyond just creating superior products; he aims to inspire players and fans alike to strive for personal growth and improvement in all aspects of life. Through Bruce Bolt, Bear embodies the spirit of "Be Better," encouraging everyone to push their limits and make the most of every opportunity to compete and excel.

About the compony  

Bruce Bolt

 

Bruce Bolt is a family-owned and operated company based in Austin, Texas, founded in 2017 by Bear Mayer, a 16-year-old baseball player. The company was born out of Bear's desire for better-quality batting gloves and the need for funds to purchase his first vehicle, a '93 Landcruiser named Jennifer. Inspired by his grandfather, Bruce, an exceptional athlete and baseball player, and driven by the mantra "Be Better," Bruce Bolt aims to create the highest quality, longest-lasting equipment in baseball, backed by excellent customer service. The company's name, combining "Bruce" with "Bolt," reflects both the influence of Bear's grandfather and the resilience and energy associated with being struck by lightning twice, a feat Bear's grandfather accomplished. Bruce Bolt distinguishes itself from competitors by focusing on quality over cost-cutting measures. Unlike many brands that prioritize synthetic materials and lower-cost production methods, Bruce Bolt uses premium leather and innovative designs to ensure durability and comfort. This commitment to excellence has attracted a wide range of customers, from youth and high school players to minor and major league athletes, with over 30 MLB and more than 150 minor league players currently using their products. As the brand continues to grow, it faces the challenge of scaling while maintaining its core values and commitment to quality. The company's journey began with Bear's frustration over the short lifespan of expensive batting gloves and evolved into a mission to create superior baseball equipment.
